Lake Glenville's Hidden Gem

This intimate, luxury lakefront community offers a rare chance to experience lakeside living like never before, with this homesite offering just over an acre of wooded privacy.

Your Deeded Boat Slip Awaits

Each homesite includes a deeded boat slip on the lake, with community perks like pickleball, hatchet throwing, trails, a vintage barn for gatherings, and future plans for a lakeside clubhouse by the docks.

Pre-Paid Membership to GlenCove by Old Edwards

Tocami is also the only community where you receive a pre-paid membership to GlenCove by Old Edwards (annual dues not included), giving you access to a 12-hole par 3 golf course, trout ponds, wellness center, community gardens, sports courts, a pool with splash pad, and the action-packed Entertainment Barn with dining and more.
