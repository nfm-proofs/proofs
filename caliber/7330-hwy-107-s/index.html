A Mountain Cottage Built to Last — On 5.1 Acres Bordered by Conserved Wilderness

Surrounded by Conservation Land, Minutes from Town

It's the kind of setting that feels remote, yet everything you need in Cashiers is a short drive away.

Whether you're looking for a private retreat or a full-time residence, the layout delivers everything you need with room to grow.

Room to Grow, Freedom to Build
